About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!


My name is Natalia, and I am a strength and fitness athlete from Canada. I am 155 cm (5'1") tall and have dedicated many years of my life to building strength, athletic performance, and a physique that reflects my passion for training.


Fitness is more than a hobby — it is a lifestyle. Through years of consistent training, discipline, and dedication, I have developed a physique that combines muscularity, strength, athleticism, and mobility.

My Strength Journey

Strength training has been my passion for many years. For me, training is about much more than building muscle — it represents discipline, consistency, determination, and the continuous pursuit of self-improvement.


Standing at 155 cm (5'1") with a defined body weight of 75 kg (165 lbs), I have dedicated countless hours to developing strength, muscularity, athletic performance, and overall physical conditioning.


My strongest body parts have always been my legs, calves, back, and shoulders. I enjoy combining heavy strength training, athletic conditioning, and mobility work to create a physique that is both powerful and balanced.
